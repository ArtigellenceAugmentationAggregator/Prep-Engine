01 / What AAA Actually Is

An intelligence layer, not a record store. An orchestrator, not a model.

AAA does not replace your doctor, lawyer or accountant. It does not store your data. It does not train on what you upload. It is a sovereign cognitive pipeline that lives for ten seconds, prepares you, and disappears — so the conversation with the human expert is finally a good one.

↳ Doctrine

The world has built remarkable AI tools for experts. Doctors get ambient scribes. Lawyers get discovery engines. Bankers get compliance copilots. None of these tools sit in the hand of the person walking into the room.

That asymmetry is the entire problem. The expert has minutes. You have a folder. The expert speaks in jargon. You speak in worry. The expert needs structure. You arrive with chaos. The fifteen minutes that follow are predictable: missed context, missed questions, missed risks.

AAA closes that gap by being narrow on purpose. It does not give advice. It does not make decisions. It does not file claims, draft contracts or write prescriptions. It does one thing: it takes everything you have — typed, scanned, photographed, recorded, scribbled — and returns a single brief that makes the next conversation worth having.

Built on a multi-model consensus layer over frontier APIs. Stateless by architecture. Premium by execution. Yours by design.

All processing routes through enterprise frontier APIs configured for zero data retention. Anthropic and Google contractually guarantee that data dispatched through commercial API endpoints is never persisted, never reviewed, never used to train future models.

↳ FAQ · 12 cards
↳ FAQ 01
How accurate is AAA on handwritten prescriptions?
Realistic baseline is 92–98% legibility accuracy. Frontier vision models extract raw text; domain databases cross-reference for plausibility. Anything below threshold is flagged amber, not silently smoothed.
↳ FAQ 02
Why not run this locally on my device?
Local edge processing breaks on the real workload. A 30-page scanned PDF plus a voice memo needs frontier-grade vision and reasoning. Zero retention is about whether data survives the session, not where it processes.
↳ FAQ 03
If you store nothing, what stops subpoenas?
A subpoena can only compel what exists. Ten seconds after your brief renders, there is nothing on our servers to compel. Frontier APIs operate under contractual ZDR. The chain has no link to seize.
↳ FAQ 04
How is this different from ChatGPT?
Three structural differences. Multi-model consensus (not one model). ZDR by architecture (not by toggle). Premium artifact output (not a markdown wall). Generic chatbots give cognition. AAA gives a finished brief.
↳ FAQ 05
Can my firm white-label AAA?
Yes — Professional tier. Your logo, your colours, your domain on every output. Same stateless architecture. The watermark loop quietly seeds referrals as clients share their briefs.
↳ FAQ 06
What if the AI tech changes?
AAA is a consensus orchestrator, not a model wrapper. When new frontier models ship, AAA swaps the engine and keeps the wiring. Your workflow doesn't move. Output stays consistent.
↳ FAQ 07
Will this replace doctors and lawyers?
No. The opposite. AAA exists to make time with a real professional worth what you pay. Without prep, half the appointment is admin. With prep, it's all strategy. The expert keeps their job. You stop wasting it.
↳ FAQ 08
Who owns the brief AAA generates?
You — completely. The HTML, PDF, audio. AAA generates, streams to your device, erases its own copy. No archive on our side. Save it, or it's gone.
↳ FAQ 09
What if AAA gets something wrong?
Confidence flags surface ambiguity instead of hiding it (first line). Disclaimers are explicit, enforced by acknowledgment gateway (second). Everything verifies with a licensed professional before action.
↳ FAQ 10
How fast is "ten seconds" really?
Standard task with 5–10 documents under 20MB: 8–12 seconds end-to-end. Heavy multimodal (video + 100+ pages): 30–60 seconds. Either way, faster than reading the folder.
↳ FAQ 11
Do you accept UPI / non-Stripe payments?
Yes. Stripe globally, UPI for India, PayPal as global fallback, Apple Pay and Google Pay for biometric checkout. The payment processor is fully isolated from the document engine.
↳ FAQ 12

Operated from Sydney, Australia. Serves globally — the engine is jurisdiction-aware (drug indices, legal databases shift per region) but the architecture is universal. Pricing in USD. Disclaimers localise per session.
